About Us

TAMS (Toledo Atlas Manpower Services) provides skilled manpower support for port operations and industrial needs. Our workforce includes stevedores, winch operators, payloader drivers, palletizers, arrastre, kapatas, mooring crews, and more.

Based in Toledo City, Cebu, we are a trusted partner of Atlas Fertilizer and other clients, ensuring smooth and efficient operations with reliable manpower.
